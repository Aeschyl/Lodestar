FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is this app?

This app is called Attract and enables tourists to easily find points of interest in the area increasing the time spent on having fun and discovering new places

 
What problem does it solve?

Tourists often visit a city but end up not knowing where to go or visit. This app simplifies and speeds up the search for such attractions

 
How do I use it?

Click find a place and go through the screens that help you narrow your search. Eventually you reach the end where the results are displayed on an interactive map

 
What data does this app store about me?

The only data linked to you is the places you have visited or plan to visit

 
Do I need to specify subcategory while finding a place?

No, you do not, but it is recommended for more refined results
